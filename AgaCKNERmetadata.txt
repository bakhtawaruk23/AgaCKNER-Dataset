AgaNER Dataset Metadata
=======================

DATASET INFORMATION
------------------
Name: AgaCorpus - Kurdish Sorani Named Entity Recognition Dataset
Version: 1.0.0
Release Date: 2024
Language: Kurdish Sorani (ckb)
Domain: News Articles
Size: 64,563 tokens | 2,534 sentences
Format: CoNLL (token - BIO-tag)

DATA COLLECTION
--------------
Primary Source: Rudaw Media Network (www.rudaw.net)
Collection Period: January 2023 - December 2023
News Categories:
- Kurdistan News
- Middle East News
- World News
- Economic News
- Sports News

PREPROCESSING PIPELINE
---------------------
1. Text Extraction & Cleaning
   - HTML tag removal
   - Non-Kurdish character filtering
   - Whitespace  

2. Kurdish-Specific Processing
   - KLPT (Kurdish Language Processing Toolkit) application
   - English numeral conversion to Kurdish
   - Sentence segmentation
   - Tokenization optimization

ANNOTATION SCHEMA
----------------
Entity Types:
- PERSON: Individual names
- LOCATION: Geographic locations, cities, countries
- ORGANIZATION: Companies, institutions, agencies
- DATE: Temporal expressions
- MISCELLANEOUS: Other named entities (Historical, Scientific Names, Sports, Currency, Unique Names, Drugs) 

BIO Tagging:
   ---
B-[TYPE]: Beginning of entity
I-[TYPE]: Continuation of entity
O: Not part of any entity

QUALITY ASSURANCE
----------------
Annotation Process:
- Initial annotation by Kurdish NLP specialists
- Review by native Kurdish Sorani speakers
- Cross-validation by linguistic experts
- Consistency checks via automated tools
- Manual error correction and standardization
